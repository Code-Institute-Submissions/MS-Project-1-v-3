Why choose Active Kids after-school club?

We offer a wide range of sport activities at our centre

We know that it can be a challenge for kids to get enough daily physical activity. Our centre has the facilities to keep your kids moving whatever the weather.

We provide after-school collection service

Our service includes an option to collect your child from nearby schools.

We provide nutritious snacks and hot meals

Your child will be offered nutritious meal prepared by our on-site chef. We use only fresh ingredients that are locally sourced.

Call us if you would like to find out more. Alternatively, you can submit your query here and one of our stuff member will get in touch with you.

All of our sports activities are led by certified professionals who have years of experience working with young children.
